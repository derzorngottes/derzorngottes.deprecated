current projects

untitled (in dreams)

A musical instrument puzzle is built based on an Arduino. Only certain parts of the instrument are available at first. The instrument is connected to a virtual reality world built in Unity, where the player must communicate with the environment and its inhabitants in order to unlock new functions in the instrument. Playing the instrument in different ways influences the virtual world and the characters the player can interact with. In this way, the player must achieve symbiosis between the instrument and the world in which it dreams.

status: initial prototype built connecting arduino controller to unity environment; simple procedural environment created in unity.

other work

plantasia

Using a newly-developed sensor from Swiss company Phytl Signs, the electrical signals are recorded from a house plant's leaves as it responds to its environment. These signals are then processed into musical tracks using the Supercollider programming language and predefined synth voices. The result is an ambient melody signalling the plant's feelings about its health and environment.

infinite garden

A virtual procedurally-generated garden full of evolving, cross-pollinating plants. Taking the idea of a true 'genetic algorithm,' a simple genome is created for plants in the virtual world. The plants have certain requirements for light, altitude, soil type, and water proximity. Healthier plants are more likely to pollinate; the wind carries germinated seeds to new locations where they may take root if the conditions are favorable.

hiv medfinder

A simple web app providing low-income/no-insurance HIV patients with access to medication coupons, a locator for low-cost medication, and links to nearby clinics for low-cost treatment.
